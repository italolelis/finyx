**6 numbered sections per D-06:**

**## 1. GKV (Gesetzliche Krankenversicherung)**
- ### 1.1 Contribution Formula — employee path: `monthly_gross_capped = min(gross_monthly_income, 5812.50)`, `gkv_employee = monthly_gross_capped * (0.073 + zusatzbeitrag / 2)`, `pv_employee = monthly_gross_capped * pv_rate` (0.019 with children, 0.025 childless 23+), `total = gkv_employee + pv_employee`
- ### 1.2 Beitragsbemessungsgrenze (BBG) — €69,750/yr, €5,812.50/mo. Emphasize: BBG is the CONTRIBUTION CAP, NOT the PKV eligibility threshold (that is JAEG in section 4)
- ### 1.3 Zusatzbeitrag — table with: GKV-Spitzenverband average 2.9%, range 2.18%-4.4%, `fallback_rate: 2.9%`, `source_url: https://www.gkv-spitzenverband.de` (per D-04, D-05). NO individual fund rates.
- ### 1.4 Pflegeversicherung (PV) — PV employee 1.9% (with children), employer 1.7%, total 3.6%. Childless 23+ surcharge +0.6% (employee only, total 4.2%). Reduction -0.25% per child (2nd+ under 25), min 2.4% at 5+ children.
- ### 1.5 Employer Contribution Caps (per D-07, inline here) — for PKV employees: KV cap €508.59/mo, PV cap €104.63 (non-Saxony) / €75.56 (Saxony), combined €613.22 non-Saxony. Formula: `employer_subsidy = min((pkv_monthly + ppv_monthly) / 2, 508.59 + 104.63)`
- Self-employed path: pays BOTH shares (no Arbeitgeberzuschuss), `gkv_self = monthly_gross_capped * (0.146 + zusatzbeitrag)`, `pv_self = monthly_gross_capped * pv_rate_full`

**## 2. PKV (Private Krankenversicherung)**
- ### 2.1 Age-Based Premium Estimation — table of indicative ranges by entry age: 25 (€250-350), 30 (€350-500), 35 (€450-650), 40 (€550-800), 45 (€700-1000), 50 (€900-1200+). Label clearly as "indicative benchmarks, not quotes"
- ### 2.2 Altersrückstellungen — explain aging provisions mechanism briefly
- ### 2.3 Beitragsrückerstattung — premium refund for no-claims years
- ### 2.4 Selbstbeteiligung — deductible options that reduce monthly premium
- ### 2.5 Long-Term Projection Constants — table: PKV growth conservative 5%, base 6%, optimistic 3%. GKV BBG growth conservative 2%, base 3%, optimistic 4%. Include NOTE about 2026 actual 7-12% PKV increases due to Krankenhausreform — conservative 5% understates current cycle, revisit if actual exceeds 8%/year.

**## 3. Familienversicherung**
- ### 3.1 GKV Free Coverage Rules — non-working partner (income < €603/mo) and children: €0 additional premium. Explicit: `total_family_gkv = total_gkv_cost_to_employee` (no multiplier)
- ### 3.2 PKV Per-Person Costs — each family member needs own policy. Children €150-200/mo, partner €300-600/mo. Side-by-side comparison showing GKV family advantage.

**## 4. Eligibility Thresholds & Risk Tiers**
- ### 4.1 JAEG and BBG Thresholds — JAEG new switchers €77,400/yr (§6 SGB V), existing PKV holders threshold €69,750 (= BBG). Explain two-threshold structure clearly. Cross-reference section 1.2 for BBG contribution cap usage.
- ### 4.2 3-Tier PKV Risk Model (per D-01, D-03) — ~15 binary flags (yes/no only, no diagnosis details). Tier 0: 0 flags = 0% surcharge. Tier 1: 1-2 minor controlled conditions = 10-25%. Tier 2: 3+ flags or any high-rejection flag = 30-50%+. Include the binary flag list (~15 conditions): diabetes, hypertension, asthma/COPD, back/spinal conditions, joint conditions, thyroid disorders, allergies (severe), sleep apnea, elevated BMI (>30), regular medication, previous surgery (last 5 years), previous hospitalization (last 5 years), chronic skin conditions, gastrointestinal conditions, migraines/chronic headaches.
- ### 4.3 Rejection-Risk Callout (per D-02) — marked as "high rejection probability" advisory note: mental health history (depression, anxiety, therapy), HIV/AIDS, active cancer/remission <5 years, cardiac events (heart attack, stroke), autoimmune disorders (MS, Crohn's, lupus). These are NOT tiered — they often result in outright rejection or prohibitive surcharges.

**## 5. §10 EStG Deduction (Basisabsicherung)**
- ### 5.1 Employee Cap — €1,900/year
- ### 5.2 Self-Employed Cap — €2,800/year
- ### 5.3 ELStAM Electronic Reporting — note 2026 change: insurers transmit data to BZSt electronically, employees no longer self-report
- Formula: `basisabsicherung_portion = pkv_monthly * 0.85` (conservative, range 70-90%), `annual_deductible = min(basisabsicherung_portion * 12, cap)`, `annual_tax_benefit = annual_deductible * marginal_rate`, `net_pkv_monthly = pkv_monthly - (annual_tax_benefit / 12)`

**## 6. Special Cases**
- ### 6.1 Beamter / Beihilfe Redirect — short redirect note (per pension.md pattern): state pays 50-80% via Beihilfe, standard GKV/PKV models do NOT apply, redirect when `employment.type == "beamter"`, full model deferred to v1.3 (BEAM-01)
- ### 6.2 Expat Anwartschaft — explain Anwartschaftsversicherung for temporary relocation, EU portability notes, non-EU gap risks
- ### 6.3 Age-55 Lock-In Warning (§6 Abs. 3a SGB V) — use a blockquote WARNING block (not plain text). After age 55, returning to GKV is effectively impossible even if income drops below JAEG. Phase 10/11 commands should check user age.

**Footer:** source citation line matching existing doc convention.
